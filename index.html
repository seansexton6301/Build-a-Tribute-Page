Eddie Van Halen

The man who revolutionized rock guitar

Here's a timeline of Eddie Van Halen's life:

  • 1955 - Born Edward Lodewijk Van Halen in Amsterdam, Netherlands
  • 1962 - Family immigrates to Pasadena, California with very little money
  • 1964-1967 - Wins piano competitions; never learns to read music, plays by ear
  • 1972 - Forms band Mammoth with his brother Alex
  • 1974 - David Lee Roth joins as singer; band renamed Van Halen
  • 1977 - Signs with Warner Bros. Records
  • 1978 - Debut album *Van Halen* released; "Eruption" introduces two-handed tapping to the world
  • 1981 - Marries actress Valerie Bertinelli
  • 1982 - Plays the iconic guitar solo on Michael Jackson’s “Beat It”
  • 1984 - Album *1984* explodes with #1 hit “Jump”
  • 1991 - Son Wolfgang Van Halen is born
  • 2007 - Van Halen inducted into the Rock & Roll Hall of Fame
  • 2008 - Gets sober after long battle with alcoholism
  • 2009 - Marries Janie Liszewski
  • 2012 - Releases *A Different Kind of Truth* with original lineup
  • 2020 - Passes away on October 6 at age 65 after battle with cancer

"I never took a guitar lesson. I just listened to records and tried to figure it out myself."

— Eddie Van Halen
